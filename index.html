My Love for Coffee 04 August 2024

I love coffee more than anything. I drink coffee at least three times a day. I think it gives me a boost of energy and makes my day more enthusiastic. It contains caffeine, which provides a sense of alertness. I would love to try Starbucks coffee and plan to do so soon. There are many types of coffee, but I prefer a simple cup with milk, regular coffee powder, and sugar. However, I know that coffee can have chemicals that may affect the brain, so I try to limit the number of cups I drink.
